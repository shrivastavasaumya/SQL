

Enhanced Entity-Relationship (EER) diagram 

This model represents the logical structure of a database, including entities, their attributes, and relationships between them.
Concepts like inheritance, specialization, generalization, and union types. 
Act as physical data models that map directly to actual SQL tables, columns, primary keys, and foreign key relationships.
